What is a Text Case Converter?

A text case converter is an online tool that allows you to change the letter casing of any text easily. Whether you want to convert your text to uppercase, lowercase, title case, or sentence case, this tool makes the process fast and convenient.

How to Use the Text Case Converter?

Simply paste or type your text into the input box above, then click one of the buttons to transform your text into the desired case. The character counter updates in real-time, helping you keep track of your text length.

What Are Different Text Cases?

  • Uppercase: Converts all letters to capital letters.
  • Lowercase: Converts all letters to small letters.
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word.
  • Sentence Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence, including after periods, exclamation marks, and question marks.

Why Use a Character Counter?

A character counter is useful for writers, students, and social media users to ensure their text fits within limits, such as Twitter posts or form fields. This tool provides instant feedback on the number of characters as you type or convert your text.

Other Useful Text Conversions

  • Binary: Converts your text into a stream of binary (0s and 1s).
  • Base64: Encodes your text in Base64, often used in data transmission.
  • Reverse: Reverses the entire text string, useful for fun or testing.
  • Hexadecimal: Converts your text into hexadecimal format, often used in programming.
  • URL Encode: Encodes your text for safe transmission in URLs, replacing spaces and special characters with their percent-encoded equivalents.
